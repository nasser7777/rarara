FRANCIS PULISI

CHAIRMAN

Dear Tirinyi Bee Keepers, I'm thrilled to share our success in managing 101 thriving bee hives, a testament to our dedication to sustainability. Our future goals include expanding to 500 hives, producing 10,000 liters of honey every six months, and reaching an annual production of 20,000 liters. These targets underscore our commitment to economic empowerment, environmental sustainability, and community development. Grateful for our supporters, together, we're creating a buzz in the world of beekeeping, echoing empowerment, sustainability, and a brighter future.

We're committed to inclusivity, making sustainable beekeeping a possibility for all. Join us in this shared journey towards empowerment and environmental sustainability!.
